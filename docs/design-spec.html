Section 4

Interaction Patterns

Documented behaviors for hover, active, loading, and empty states across the application.

Hover States

Interactive items shift border color from #2a2a35 to #3a3a45 on hover. Buttons darken slightly. Ghost elements reveal background.

Loading States (Skeleton Screens)

Skeleton placeholders with a shimmer animation while data loads.

Empty States

Shown when a section has no data. Contains a visual indicator, message, and call to action.

Detail Panel Transition

The detail panel slides in from the right edge over 200ms with an ease-out timing function. The background is dimmed with a semi-transparent overlay.

→
Step 2: BG dims, panel enters
→
Step 3: Panel fully visible
